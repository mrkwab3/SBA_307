Mockjup

What is mockjup?

Before we can talk about what mockjup is, we must first discuss where it came from Within the crypto community, there exists many different tokens or sub communities. One of these communities is called Solana. Solana has the advantage of taking less fees than its competitors (bitcoin and ethereum) while also being fast and efficient. Within the Solana ecosystem, there exists a decentralized exchange (DEX) called Jupiter that allows users to trade different tokens in one streamlined process through the use of Automated Market Makers (AMMs). When Jupiter was first preparing to make its own token called JUP, it first launched a test token named mockJUP. Many users of Jupiter received mockJUP tokens for free and were surprised to find out that it had liquidity (it could be sold for real money). A community formed around the token and although it is nowhere near its peak price, mockJUP will always have a special place in our hearts for its story.
